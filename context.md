Joint replacement surgeries, such as hip and knee replacements, help restore mobility and reduce pain caused by arthritis or injury. India’s orthopedic surgeons use state-of-the-art implants and minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery.
Treatment Success Rate in India: Approximately 85-95% success rate in joint replacement surgeries, with excellent postoperative care.

India provides advanced diagnostic tools and innovative neurosurgical procedures for effective treatment.
Treatment Success Rate in India: Around 80-90%, depending on the complexity of the neurological condition treated.

Treatment Success Rate in India: Varies by cancer type, with up to 70-80% survival rates in common cancers due to early detection and advanced treatment.

Treatment Success Rate in India: Over 85% success with minimally invasive procedures and early intervention in digestive diseases.
